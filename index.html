    About Us

    Welcome to Green Tea, where nature meets tranquility in every sip. We believe that tea is more than just a beverage , it’s a way to relax, refresh, and reconnect with yourself. Our journey began with a love for pure, natural flavors and a desire to share the timeless goodness of tea with the world. Every leaf we use is carefully selected and blended to ensure freshness, quality, and authentic taste. From the vibrant energy of Matcha to the soothing comfort of Honey Lemon, each cup tells a story of care, balance, and wellness. At Green Tea, we’re passionate about promoting a healthy lifestyle through natural ingredients and mindful brewing. Whether you’re starting your morning with a refreshing brew or winding down after a long day, we’re here to make every moment a little more peaceful and refreshing.
